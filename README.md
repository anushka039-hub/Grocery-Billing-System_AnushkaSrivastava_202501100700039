# Grocery-Billing-System_AnushkaSrivastava_202501100700039
## Problem Statement
A grocery store wants to calculate the total cost of items purchased by a customer.
The system accepts prices of 3 items and applies a 10% discount if the total exceeds $50.

## Approach
- Take input for 3 item prices
- Calculate total cost
- Apply 10% discount if total > $50
- Display original total, discount, and final payable amount

## Sample Output
Enter the price of item 1: 15
Enter the price of item 2: 20
Enter the price of item 3: 35


GROCERY BILL :- 
(Discount is applicable only if the total amount exceeds 50$)
Orignal total cost: $ 70
Discount: $ 7.0
Final total amount after discount: $ 63.0
